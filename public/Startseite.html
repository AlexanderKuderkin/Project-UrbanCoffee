About us

Our Story

We are two business informatics
students who decided to combine
our passion for coffee with our
technical skills. What started as a
personal quest for the perfect cup
quickly turned into an idea we
wanted to share with others. With
this project, we've created a platform
where coffee lovers and newcomers
alike can discover and review high-
quality coffee products. Our mission
is to make the world of coffee a bit
more accessible and transparent –
for us, for you, for everyone!

Our Goal

Our goal is to build the ultimate
destination for coffee enthusiasts.
We aim to provide a trustworthy
platform where users can find honest
reviews and recommendations to
discover the perfect coffee for their
taste. At the same time, we’re
committed to promoting responsible
consumption by offering transparent
information about coffee
sustainability, origin, and quality.
Whether you're a casual coffee
drinker or a true connoisseur, you’ll
find the right insights here to elevate
your coffee experience.
